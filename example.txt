Greatsword - Blade
2 Meters
1d10 Slashing/Piercing PT
Momentum, Stance(Piercing), Long, Heavy
You can take an action to build momentum. The next time you hit with this weapon it does a bonus 50% damage.
Allows it to change its base damage to the specified damage type.
The weapon gains 1m of hit range. This is automatically calculated into non-customs.
The weapon takes up two hand slots to use.
